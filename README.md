# EcoLearn – Gamified Environmental Learning Platform

## Overview

**EcoLearn** is an interactive web/mobile platform designed to make environmental education practical, engaging, and impactful for students. It moves beyond textbook-based learning by combining gamification, real-world challenges, and behavioral tracking to promote sustainable habits.

The platform aligns with NEP 2020 and global sustainability goals by encouraging experiential learning and environmental responsibility among students.

---

## Problem Statement

Environmental education in many Indian schools remains largely **theoretical**, with:

* Minimal real-world application
* Low student engagement
* Lack of awareness about local environmental issues
* No system to track or reward sustainable behavior

As a result, students often fail to develop **long-term eco-friendly habits**.

---

## Solution

EcoLearn addresses this gap through:

* Gamified Learning Modules
* Eco-point Tracking System
* Real-world Environmental Challenges
* Rewards and Recognition System

---

## Features

### Interactive Learning

* Bite-sized lessons on:

  * Climate change
  * Waste management
  * Water conservation
  * Sustainable living

* Quiz-based progression

---

### Challenges and Tasks

* Daily/weekly real-world activities:

  * Plant a tree
  * Practice waste segregation
  * Reduce plastic use

---

### Eco Points System

* Earn points for:

  * Completing lessons
  * Finishing challenges

* Leaderboard for schools/classes

---

### Rewards and Badges

* Digital badges for achievements
* Recognition for top performers

---

### School-Level Engagement

* Eco-club integration
* Inter-school competitions

## How It Works

1. User signs up/logs in
2. Selects learning modules
3. Completes lessons and quizzes
4. Performs real-world eco tasks
5. Earns eco-points
6. Competes on leaderboard
